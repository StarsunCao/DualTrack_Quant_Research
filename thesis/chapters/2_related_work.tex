\section{Research Gaps and Positioning}

Despite significant progress in both ML and LLM approaches to trading, several gaps remain:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Lack of systematic comparison between ML and LLM paradigms
    \item Limited research on LLM performance during market stress
    \item Insufficient attention to the trade-offs between performance and cost
\end{enumerate}

This thesis addresses these gaps through the Dual-Track framework.
